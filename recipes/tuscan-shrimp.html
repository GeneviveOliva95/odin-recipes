Tuscan Shrimp

Description

Creamy Tuscan shrimp is a delicious Italian-inspired dish featuring plump, tender shrimp cooked in a rich and creamy sauce made with garlic, sun-dried tomatoes, spinach, and Parmesan cheese. It is typically served over pasta or with crusty bread to soak up all the savory flavors. This dish is known for its decadent creaminess and delightful blend of flavors that make it a favorite among seafood lovers.

Ingredients

Shrimp

  • 1 pound large shrimp, raw, deveined, tail removed
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Sauce

  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 small shallot, diced
  • 1/4 cup sun-dried tomatoes, softened in water according to package instructions, drained, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream, room temperature
  • 1/4 cup parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 2 cups baby spinach
  • Parsley, chopped for garnish
  • Salt, to taste

Steps

  1. Season shrimp evenly with salt and pepper
  2. To a large skillet over medium-high heat, add oil. Once hot, add shrimp and cook about 2-3 minutes per side. Transfer the cooked shrimp to a plate and tent to keep warm. Set aside.
  3. Reduce heat to medium and add butter. Once melted, add shallots and sun-dried tomatoes. Cook until the shallots are softened (2-4 minutes). Add garlic and cook for 1 more minute. Sprinkle flour over the top and stir to combine.
  4. Add heavy cream, parmesan cheese, and cherry tomatoes. Stir to combine, scraping up the browned bits from the bottom of the pan as you go.
  5. Reduce heat to low. Simmer until the cheese is fully melted and the sauce has thickened slightly, about 3-4 minutes.
  6. Add spinach and stir to combine. Cook for 2-3 more minutes, or until the spinach is wilted.
  7. Top with cooked shrimp. Serve immediately garnished with parsley and extra parmesan cheese.
