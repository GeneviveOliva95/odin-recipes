Chicken Tetrazzini

Description

Chicken tetrazzini is a creamy pasta dish made with diced chicken, mushrooms, and a rich sauce typically made with cream, broth, and Parmesan cheese. It is often baked until bubbly and served as a comforting and hearty meal. The dish is believed to have been named after Italian opera singer Luisa Tetrazzini in the early 20th century.

Ingredients

  • 10 ounce can cream of chicken soup
  • 10 ounce can cream of mushroom soup
  • 2 cups sour cream
  • 1/2cup low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas
  • 1 pound seasoned cooked chicken, chopped or shredded
  • 1 pound thin spaghetti, cooked al dente
  • 1 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 tablespoons grated parmesan cheese
  • Fresh chopped parsley, for garnish

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Coat a 9 by 13 baking dish with nonstick cooking spray, set aside.
  2. In large bowl whisk together both soups, sour cream, butter, chicken broth, salt, pepper, and garlic powder until combined.
  3. Stir in the peas, chicken, and spaghetti; toss to combine until fully coated.
  4. Pour mixture into the prepared baking dish and sprinkle with the cheeses.
  5. Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes, then remove foil and continue baking for another 15 minutes until cheese is melted and casserole is hot throughout.
  6. Remove from oven and sprinkle with parsley. Let stand 5-10 minutes.
  7. Serve and enjoy!
